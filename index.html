Forgotten Future

Project Introduction

Welcome to the Forgotten Future Project Page. Here you'll find information on the upcoming open source parallax scrolling action/adventure game Forgotten Future. While you're here, feel free to listen to the Forgotten Future Title Theme and check out some of the concept art.

Forgotten Future will be officially launched in late 2018 and will be free to play. Console ports will become available thereafter depending on development time. In order to get us there (and faster) consider making a donation. All donation funds are spent (wisely) on development and concept art.

Also don't forget to subscribe to browser updates below so you'll know when Forgotten Future is launched!
