Forgotten Future

Project Introduction

Welcome to the Forgotten Future Project Page. You'll find information on the upcoming open source parallax scrolling action/adventure game Forgotten Future. Feel free to listen to the Forgotten Future Title Theme and check out some of the concept art. Don't forget to read up on the coming cataclysm...

Forgotten Future will be officially launched in late 2018 and will be free to play. Console ports will become available thereafter depending on development time. In order to get us there (and faster) consider making a donation. All donation funds are spent (wisely) on essential concept art.

While you're here don't forget to subscribe to browser updates so you'll know when Forgotten Future is launched!
