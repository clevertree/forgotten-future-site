Frequently Asked Questions

What is Forgotten Future?

Forgotten Future is an upcoming open source video game built in Javascript and WebGL.

What kind of game is it?

A parallax scrolling video game, which means fast-paced side-scrolling action. In Forgotten Future you will experience:

  • Exploration from boundless landscapes to deep undergrounds
  • Intense platformer fighting action involving weapons, hand-to-hand combat, and vehicle warfare

What are the System Requirements?

Forgotten Future will run on any modern browser that supports WebGL. A dedicated graphics card is recommended to take advantage of shader performance.
